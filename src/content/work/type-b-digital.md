## Overview

At Type B Digital (remote, Canada) I lead end-to-end design for a high-net-worth
portfolio management platform spanning web and mobile, driving product strategy
from concept to execution.

## Highlights

- Revamped 3 products on a unified, scalable design system — bringing consistency
  and faster cross-product development within a two-month window.
- Built and scaled a high-performing team of 6 designers across product and marketing,
  and established a structured internship program to grow junior talent.
- Guided both product and marketing design directions, keeping them aligned with
  business goals and brand strategy.
- Earlier, as Senior Product Designer, ran in-depth UX audits for a real-estate
  platform (25% lift in engagement) and designed an AI-powered event management
  platform from the ground up.
